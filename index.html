Origins

Origins:

GTA Surfing Club is a University Sporting Organisation that was formed in 1950 to establish, guide and promote the development of surfing in Australia for University students

GTA Surfing Club is the representative body on the Inter-University Surfing Association (IUSA) of which there are 10 member Universities and is recognised by the Australian Sports Commission, the Australian Olympic Committee and is a member of the Water Safety Council of Australia.

Surfing underpins an important part of the Australian coastal fabric. It forms part of a lifestyle in which millions participate with a mystique in which millions more have a documented interest


The Beginnings:

GTA Surfing Club was established to guide and promote the development of the sport for University students. The organisation was formed in a time when surfing was not a popular past-time and in an era when there was no such thing as a surfing industry or funding to support it.


The Growth:

What followed was a far more strategic approach towards the development of the sport. The framework and structure created has allowed surfing to grow both as a sport and as a lifestyle pursuit into the new millennium. The club has gotten much stronger, according to incomplete statistics, almost all Universities in Australia have been the "Group membership".
